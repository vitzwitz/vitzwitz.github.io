Hello, I'm Bri!

I am a python developer, a manager, and an aspiring numerical mathematician. I graduate Rochester Institute of Technology with a bachelor of science in applied mathematics. I am a member of the Structural Biology Extensible Visualization Scripting Language research team. We are updating the back-end of a software plug-in, ProMOL, so that we can convert it into a stand-alone version and allow it to be independent of PyMOL, a molecular visualization program. I write algorithms, apply data science techniques and libraries, and test my program by mining data from an online database, the Protein Data Bank. Math and programming are my passions, and I hope to explore a variety of fields throughout my career such as data science, software engineering, security, robotics, research, game design, and web and app development.
